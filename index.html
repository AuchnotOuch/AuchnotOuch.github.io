About Me

Hi! My name is Alex Auch, a full stack software engineer living in Montana with a passion for technology and creating new things. My journey began in the military as an electronic warfare technician on C-130s, which sparked my interest in tech. After spending a decade in the service industry, I decided to pursue an education in Information Technology. However, it was programming that truly ignited my creativity. I attended App Academy’s 6+ month coding bootcamp, where I honed my skills in JavaScript, Python, React, Express, and Flask to become a full stack developer. Today, I channel my passion for tech into engineering products and services that improve users’ lives and make a positive impact.
