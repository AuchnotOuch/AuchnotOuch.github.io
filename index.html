About Me

Hi there! My name is Alex Auch. I am a full stack software engineer currently living in the beautiful state of Montana. All my life I've had a passion for technology and a powerful drive to create new things. I started my journey in tech when I joined the military and became an electronic warfare technician on C-130s. After my time in the military and spending about a decade working in the service industry, I decided to go back to school and pursue an education in Information Technology. While I found the field of IT to be interesting, it wasn't scratching that creative itch.

After doing more research into different fields of tech I could go into and taking an intro to programming course, I decided that becoming a software engineer was a better fit for me. I switched up gears with my education and attended App Academy's 6+ month coding bootcamp where I developed the skills, like learning Javascript, Python React, Express, and Flask, to become a full stack software engineer.

As a developer, I want to use my creativity to engineer products and services that not only benefit the lives of users, but can help change the world for the better.
